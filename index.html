Shifts N Gigs

ShiftsNGigs is meant to take the hassle out of trying to schedule for major events in the service industry. It takes in data from events going on near you and helps pad your schedule to account for any increased foot traffic in the area.

I used the Eventbrite API to use data regarding event size, location, as well as the type of event. Managers have the ability to rank the relevancy of event categories based on their establishment and my app then filters through those types of events within a mile radius of their restaurant and pads the schedule based on capacity and how relevant of an event it is. This app required me to be very flexible because the schema changed often as I added new features. My extensive use of data manipulation and API calls gave me a lot of confidence as a backend developer which I hope to further.

  • Service: Web Development
